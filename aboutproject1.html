Welcome to BatterDay!


In 2015, Batterday was established. From the moment our first cake went into the oven, we have operated with a simple vision: of creating fresh, delicious, innovative products. We considered them our 'prized pastries'. Our mission has never wavered and every day we strive to keep the promise we made to our customers on our very first day.

For the best cupcakes in Chennai visit The Batterday. With 2 retail outlets in Chennai we bake 18 flavors of cupcakes everyday. We also do customization of cupcakes and cakes for special occasions.
