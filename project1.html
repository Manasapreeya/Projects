Welcome to BatterDay!


A pioneer in the online cake shop model with associated home delivery services in Chennai, we cater for all occasions and events like weddings, birthday parties, engagement parties, and anniversaries. Cake Square Chennai brings a rich culinary experience to the subtle art of baking and cake making. Using traditional techniques and the best ingredients, we offer cakes in a delicious variety of flavours. Exceptional customer service complements our baking excellence and we go the extra mile in undertaking 'midnight cake delivery' and 'doorstep delivery' for your special occasion.


In 2015, Batterday was established. From the moment our first cake went into the oven, we have operated with a simple vision: of creating fresh, delicious, innovative products. We considered them our 'prized pastries'. Our mission has never wavered and every day we strive to keep the promise we made to our customers on our very first day. Over the years we have had the privilege of serving you in Chennai and in Bangalore with our flagship stores in Kothari Road in Chennai and Krishna Temple Road in Bangalore. We have done so with one vision in our hearts- to help you taste a memory.
